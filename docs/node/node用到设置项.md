```类型/子类型[;参数(如charset=utf-8)] * 代表取所有```

   > text/plain
   > text/xml
   > image/gif
   > image/jpeg
   > image/png
   > application/xhtml+xml
   > application/xml
   > application/atom+xml
   > application/json
   > application/pdf
   > application/msword
   > application/octet-stream
   > application/x-www-form-urlencoded  表单默认
   > multipart/form-data    文件上传
